Battery Management
Challenge: Forgetting to charge before travel or extended creative sessions limits spontaneous use.
Solution: Establish charging routines, treating your printer like a phone. Keep a portable power bank dedicated to your printer. Many models charge via standard USB, allowing charging from laptops or car ports during travel. Monitor battery levels through companion apps.
